Who We Are

We are a husband and wife team dedicated to helping you experience unique snorkeling experiences different from the common tourist attractions

Our beautiful sailing area

We do sailing/snorkeling charters about 5 1/2 miles offshore to Eastern Blue Cut, the last reefs on the edge of the Bermuda Plateau. We are also one of the only companies that go out to the Outer Reef. We especially like Blue Hole.

The Best View

The reefs we like to visit are young and healthy, and quite shallow in many places. Because the water is so clear here, diving is not necessary. The mooring at Blue Hole is in 30-40 feet of water, but spots over the reef are only about 4 feet.

For Your Safety
